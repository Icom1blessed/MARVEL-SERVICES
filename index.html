    Shipping Agency

    A shipping agency handles the operational and procedural requirements for a commercial vessel’s call at a port. This includes cargo handling, emergency calls, repairs, crew changes, and ship demolition. Shipping agencies ensure smooth and efficient port operations, protecting the interests of ship owners and charterers1.

    Protective Agency

    More details about Protective Agency services. We ensure that your assets are fully protected...Protective agencies safeguard the interests of ship owners and operators during port calls. They supervise the charterer’s agency function, monitor port call operations, and ensure compliance with local regulations.

    Ship Chandling

    Ship chandlers provide essential supplies and services to ships, including food provisions, repairs, spare parts, safety inspections, and medical supplies.

    Ship Husbanding

    Ship husbanding involves managing the affairs of a ship while in port, including customs formalities, fueling, supplies, repairs, and crew requirements. Husbanding agents ensure that all needs are met efficiently, allowing for minimal time spent in port and optimized operational efficiency56.

    Vessel Brokerage

    Vessel brokerage services involve acting as intermediaries between shipowners and charterers or buyers and sellers of vessels. Brokers facilitate the sale and purchase of ships, negotiate charter agreements, and provide market intelligence to help clients make informed decisions

    Bunkering Services

    Bunkering services provide fuel for ships, ensuring that vessels have the necessary fuel to complete their voyages. This includes the supply of different types of marine fuels, such as IFO, MGO, and MDO, as well as lubricants and other related services

    Ship Crew Security

    Ship crew security services ensure the safety and security of the crew on board. This includes providing security personnel, implementing safety protocols, and ensuring compliance with international maritime security regulations to protect against piracy and other threats2.

    Freight Fowarding

    Freight forwarding involves organizing the shipment of goods from one place to another. Freight forwarders handle all logistics, including transportation, customs clearance, and documentation, ensuring that goods are delivered efficiently and on time9.

    Service Boat Agency

    Service boat agencies provide support vessels for various maritime operations, including crew transfers, supply deliveries, and emergency response. These agencies ensure that support services are available when needed, enhancing the efficiency and safety of maritime operations5.
